# Understanding Billing Plans

**Features**:

- 1-minute check interval
- 5 team members
- Email notifications
- Basic analytics

**Features**:

- Custom headers

### Business Plan

**Price**: Starting at $199/month

**Best For**: Mid-sized companies, business-critical monitoring

- Private deployment option
- Custom integrations

**Best For**: Large enterprises, mission-critical systems

## Billing Basics

### Subscription Billing

- Billed monthly or annually
- Automatic renewal
- Cancel anytime
- Annual pricing: 20% discount

### Monthly Billing

- Charge date: Same day each month
- Invoice emailed automatically
- Payment methods: Credit card, wire transfer
- Upgrade/downgrade immediately effective

### Annual Billing

- 20% discount vs. monthly
- Charge date: Same day each year
- Cancel with 30 days notice
- Prorated refund on cancellation

## Upgrading Your Plan

### Step 1: Go to Billing Settings

1. Click **Settings > Billing**
2. Review current plan
3. Click **Upgrade**

### Step 2: Choose New Plan

1. Select desired plan
2. Review features
3. See pricing
4. Click **Upgrade**

### Step 3: Prorated Billing

- Charged difference immediately
- Remaining credit applied to next cycle
- No loss of service
- All features available immediately

### Step 4: Confirmation

- Invoice generated
- Confirmation email sent
- Features activate immediately
- Team notified

## Downgrading Your Plan

### When Downgrading

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select new plan
4. Review changes
5. Confirm downgrade

### What Happens

- Features are disabled after current billing cycle
- Excess monitors disabled (you choose which)
- Team members removed (if over limit)
- Data retained but inaccessible
- Credit applied to next cycle

### Example: Downgrade Professional → Starter

- Currently have 50 monitors → Limited to 5
- Currently have 20 team members → Limited to 5
- Choose which monitors to keep active
- Others paused (data preserved)
- Extra team members removed

## Adding Team Members

### Billing Impact

- Starter: 5 included, no additional cost
- Professional: 25 included, $5/month per additional
- Business: Unlimited included
- Enterprise: Unlimited included

### Overage Charges

- Professional plan with 30 team members
- 5 team members over limit × $5 = $25/month extra
- Pro-rated if added mid-cycle
- Applied to next invoice

## Payment Methods

### Accepted Payment Methods

- **Credit Cards**: Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- **Debit Cards**: Any major debit card
- **Bank Transfer**: Wire transfer for annual plans
- **Purchase Order**: For Enterprise customers

### Adding Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Payment Methods**
3. Click **Add Payment Method**
4. Enter card details
5. Set as default
6. Save

### Updating Payment Method

1. Go to **Payment Methods**
2. Click card to edit
3. Update details
4. Save changes

### Removing Payment Method

1. Click card
2. Click **Remove**
3. Ensure another method exists
4. Confirm removal

## Cancellation

### Canceling Subscription

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Select reason
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Data retained for 30 days

### Refunds

- No refunds for unused service
- If charged in error, contact support
- Prorated credits applied
- Process 5-10 business days

### After Cancellation

- Account downgraded to free tier
- Monitors disabled
- Team members removed
- Data retained for 30 days
- Re-enable by subscribing again
